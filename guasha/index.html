How to Use

C-Shaped Scraper (C)

Best for: back, calves, hamstrings, thighs, and arms.

Use long, smooth strokes following the natural direction of the muscle.

S-Shaped Scraper (S)

Best for: neck, shoulders, arms, legs, and curved body areas.

Use the curved edge to fit body contours. Glide with light to moderate pressure.

D-Shaped Scraper (D)

Best for: shoulders, waist, forearms, feet, and smaller muscle areas.

Hold through the grip opening for better control. Use short, steady strokes.

Crescent Scraper (M)

Best for: shoulders, back, thighs, calves, and broad curved areas.

Use the long curved edge for smooth sweeping strokes over larger sections.

Heart-Shaped Scraper (H)

Best for: cheeks, jawline, neck, and facial contours.

Glide upward and outward with gentle pressure. Avoid direct use on irritated skin.

Lip-Shaped Scraper (L)

Best for: cheeks, jawline, forehead, and delicate facial curves.

Use the smooth edge for slow facial massage. Keep strokes light and even.

Trigger Point Roller Pen (T)

Best for: eye area, temples, smile lines, and small facial points.

Roll or press gently. Use with eye cream or serum if desired.

Body Area Guide

Neck & Shoulders

Recommended tools: S, D, M. Use gentle, controlled strokes and follow natural body contours.

Back & Waist

Recommended tools: C, D, M. Use long strokes for larger areas and short strokes for focused pressure.

Arms

Recommended tools: S, C, D. Glide along the muscle direction with light to moderate pressure.

Thighs & Calves

Recommended tools: C, S, M. Use smooth sweeping strokes over larger sections.

Face & Jawline

Recommended tools: H, L. Glide upward and outward with gentle pressure only.

Eye Area & Small Points

Recommended tool: T. Roll or press gently around temples, smile lines, and small facial points.

Use Tips

  • Start with light pressure and increase gradually.
  • Use with massage oil, lotion, body cream, eye cream, or serum as appropriate.
  • Keep each body area within 1-3 minutes.
  • Move in slow, even strokes. Do not scrape aggressively.
  • Stop immediately if sharp pain, numbness, or discomfort occurs.

Care & Safety

Do Not Use On

  • Open wounds or broken skin
  • Bruises, swelling, or irritated skin
  • Varicose veins
  • Face, throat, or sensitive areas with excessive pressure
  • Areas with recent surgery

Important

This product is for external use only. Consult a healthcare professional before use if you are pregnant, have a medical condition, or are unsure whether this product is suitable for you.

Cleaning

Wash with warm water and mild soap after each use. Dry completely before storage.

Storage

Store tools in the included pouch. Keep away from children and avoid dropping the tools.
